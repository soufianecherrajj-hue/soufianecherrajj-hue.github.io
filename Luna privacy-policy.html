Section 01

Data Collection & Use

We believe in data minimization. We only collect the information necessary to provide the core functionality of our applications.

  • Information Stored Locally Our applications are designed to be "local-first" whenever possible. Your data, settings, and personal logs are stored only on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal data on our servers.
  • Third-Party Services To provide app functionality, we use third-party services such as Google Play Services and AdMob. These services may collect information such as IP addresses and device identifiers to maintain services, provide analytics, and prevent fraud. You can learn more about how Google handles this data in Google's Privacy & Terms.
  • Permissions If you grant specific device permissions (such as Location, Camera, or Storage), they are used solely for the features requested within the application. This data is not stored or shared with us unless explicitly stated otherwise.
Section 02

Data Security

We value your trust. We implement standard security measures to ensure your information remains secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data, please remember that no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure.

Section 03

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We only share data with third-party service providers (like Google) when it is required for app functionality, such as displaying advertisements or analyzing application performance.

Section 04

User Rights

  • Access or Delete Data You can clear all your information by uninstalling the app or by using the reset/delete options within the app settings.
  • Withdraw Consent You can revoke app permissions at any time through your device's system settings.
Section 05

Children's Privacy

Our applications are not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
